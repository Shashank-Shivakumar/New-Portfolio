About Me

Manisha Vaswani

Researcher | Professor | Entrepreneur

I am a researcher, professor, and entrepreneur with a global perspective on innovation and strategy. My teaching spans entrepreneurship, family business, international business, and organizational strategy, with a focus on experiential learning and student engagement. My research explores entrepreneurial storytelling, immigrant entrepreneurship, digital transformation, and the psychological dimensions of entrepreneurial mindsets, drawing on real-world narratives and diverse ecosystems. I’ve published across high-impact journals and authored multiple books on Entrepreneurship, Strategy, Business Analytics, and AI. Beyond academia, I serve as a strategic advisor to startups and nonprofits, mentor entrepreneurs through national accelerators, and lead businesses like Seed and Scale, Intellaica and CrunchFind to connect education with practical impact. I believe in building inclusive entrepreneurial systems and using technology to bridge the gap between vision and execution.
